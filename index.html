Why It Matters

Awareness is preventive medicine.

Most public-health and online-safety problems are not knowledge problems first — they're awareness and habit problems. Swasthya Connect exists to close that gap with short, specific, repeatable guidance instead of long lectures.

This portal was built as a CSP project demonstrating end-to-end design and front-end development for an awareness-driven public information site, including an in-browser assistant, "Saathi," to answer common questions instantly.

"The healthiest communities aren't the ones with the most information — they're the ones where the right information is easiest to find."
