Pillar 03 · Digital Wellness

Wellness for the hours we spend online.

Technology isn't the enemy — unexamined habits are. A few intentional limits keep devices useful without letting them run your day.

Screen Time

Manage attention, not just hours.

01

The 20-20-20 rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to ease eye strain.

02

Set app-level timers

Most phones let you cap specific apps — social media and games are the usual culprits.

03

No screens 1 hour before bed

Blue light and engaging content both delay sleep onset.

04

Phone-free zones

Keep devices out of the bedroom and away from the dinner table when possible.

Online Safety

Most online harm is preventable with a few habits.

Use a different password for every important account, ideally via a password manager, and enable two-factor authentication wherever offered.

Be cautious of urgent messages demanding money, OTPs, or personal details — legitimate organisations rarely ask for these over message or call.

Review who can see your posts, location, and contact details every few months — defaults change as apps update.

A short monthly habit — reviewing app permissions, login activity, and privacy settings — prevents most of the common digital safety issues people run into.
