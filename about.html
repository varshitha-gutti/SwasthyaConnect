About The Initiative

Why Swasthya Connect exists.

"Swasthya" means health and wellbeing. We built this portal on a simple belief: people don't lack the will to be healthy, hygienic, and digitally safe — they often lack a single, clear place to find the basics. Swasthya Connect is that place.

Our Mission

Make everyday wellness easy to act on.

Swasthya Connect distills public-health and digital-safety guidance into short, specific, repeatable actions — the kind you can actually remember the next time it matters: before a meal, before bed, or before clicking a suspicious link.

Who It's For

Students, families, and community workers.

The content is written for a general audience: school and college students researching health awareness topics, families wanting a quick reference at home, and community health workers who need simple material to share further.

How The Pillars Connect

Health, hygiene, and digital wellness reinforce each other.

🩺

Physical Health

Sleep, nutrition, and movement are the foundation — when these slip, hygiene habits and screen discipline usually slip too.

🧼

Hygiene

Hygiene is health's daily maintenance routine — small, consistent actions that prevent illness before it starts.

📱

Digital Wellness

Our devices shape sleep, mood, and even posture — digital habits are now a core part of physical and mental wellbeing.
